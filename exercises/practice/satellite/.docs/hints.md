# Hints

## General

When dealing with trees, [recursion][recursion] is often your friend.

- The key here is to find a way to break the problem down into smaller problems with the same structure.
- There is a hint in the instructions which points to where this can be done.
- Both of the two traversals are necessary for the problem.
- What is the relationship between the two traversals? The position of the `root` can offer a clue.
- What is the base case? The first test points at a good answer.

[recursion]: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Recursion
